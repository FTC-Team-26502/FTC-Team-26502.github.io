<h1>What It Is</h1>

<div>
  Students spend a full week learning <strong>robot building</strong>, <strong>programming</strong>, <strong>robot game strategy</strong>, and <strong>teamwork</strong> — all on the real <strong>FLL Unearthed field</strong>. Teams will build, code, test, and refine their robots throughout the week, wrapping up with a <strong>Friday in-camp competition</strong> on the robot game table.
  All robotics materials are provided. Our FTC team will coach and guide students every step of the way.
</div>

</br>

<h1>What Participants Will Learn</h1>

<div>
  <ul>
    <li>A reliable robot base and at least one consistent scoring routine on the FLL Unearthed table</li>
    <li>Reusable navigation and mission code (LEGO Spike blocks or Python)</li>
    <li>Refined attachments and a repeatable launch and setup process</li>
  </ul>
</div>

</br>
