<h2>Description</h2>
<p>
  Join BRB Robotics (FTC Team 26502) for a hands-on 3D Printing Workshop designed for students in grades 3–5.
  Participants will learn the basics of 3D printing, see how a printer works, and explore how teams use 3D
  printing to prototype and build parts for robotics. We’ll cover basic printer safety, common materials, and
  the steps from a digital design to a finished print. Space is limited, so please register in advance.
</p>
